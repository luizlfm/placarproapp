## 🎨 PROMPTS PARA LOGO E VARIAÇÕES

### Logo principal (variante "Pro")
> Modern sports brand logo for "PlacarPro". Design: a stylized shield-trophy hybrid in matte black with a glowing lime green (#7CC61D) lightning bolt striking through the center, soccer ball at the base, single white star at the top tip. Negative space inside the shield reveals a subtle "P" letter. Minimalist, geometric, professional. Flat vector design, clean edges. Background: pure white. Inspired by Premier League and Brasileirão emblems but more modern.

### Logo monocromático (versão sólida pra fundos coloridos)
> Monochromatic version of PlacarPro logo: solid white silhouette of a shield-trophy with a soccer ball at the base and a star at the top tip. Flat vector, no gradients, no effects. For use on dark/black backgrounds. Transparent PNG output.

### Logo "Premium/Pro" (versão dourada — pra plano pago)
> Premium "Pro" version of the PlacarPro logo. Same shield-trophy shape but with metallic gold gradient (#FFD700 to #B8860B), lime green star, embossed 3D feel with subtle drop shadow. "PRO" badge ribbon across the bottom in black with green border. 1024x1024 transparent PNG.

### Favicon / ícone redondo (perfil Instagram, WhatsApp Business)
> Circular icon for "PlacarPro" social media profile. Background: solid black (#000000). Center: a stylized lime green (#7CC61D) lightning bolt forming the letter "P", with a tiny gold star at the top-right corner. Minimal, high-contrast, scales perfectly at 32x32 to 1024x1024. Flat vector style.

### Mascote (personagem da marca — opcional)
> Friendly mascot for PlacarPro sports app: a cartoon goalkeeper character with a black jersey and lime green gloves, holding a soccer ball under one arm and a smartphone in the other hand. Confident smile, sporty build, in the style of modern brand mascots (Pringles, Duolingo). Flat illustration with soft shadows. Transparent background.
